Our Beliefs
  1. As Baptists we believe in one God who exits in three persons: Father, Son(Jesus Christ), and Holy Spirit.
  2. As a Baptist church, Bible is our ultimate authority for faith and practice.
  3. We conduct believers baptism which is done to an individual who have made a concious decision to follow Jesus Christ through immersion in water.
  4. We believe that salvation is through God's grace received by faith in Jesus Christ.
  5. As a Baptist church we are independent(separated from the state) and has the authority to govern ourselves lead by the pastor as the minister.
    6. Denomination Information

    Mwihoko Baptist Church is a religious organization under Nairobi North Baptist Association headed by the Baptist Convention of Kenya
